WHO WE ARE

Our company provides this channel to help find a home for those pets that are on the street or even in an institution.
Adopting is a conscious attitude. There will always be a pet that cannot wait to receive all the affection you want to give.

HOW YOU CAN HELP

Adopt an animal it is an act of love. We appreciate your interest in adopting a stray dog. Get ready for a rewarding experience that will last for many years.
The sponsorship of a dog is a way of obtaining fixed resources to help in the daily task of taking care of our animals, besides having more tranquility and being able to better plan our actions.
There are many ways to help us care for the more than 450 dogs that are sheltered in our Transitional Homes, apart from the dogs we help support from other protectors.
Fostering is a rewarding experience and the first step in the journey to a furever home for these animals.
